About Masjid Al-Noor

Allah Help Those Who Help Themselves

Welcome to Masjid Al-Noor, a place of worship and a hub of community service. We strive to inspire and guide people toward a life of faith and righteousness, rooted in the teachings of Islam. Our mosque offers spiritual solace, educational programs, and social services, all aimed at enhancing the well-being of the entire community.

Our Vision

We envision a spiritually vibrant community where individuals connect with their faith, deepen their knowledge of Islam, and are motivated to make a positive impact. We aim to build a society that stands strong on Islamic values and compassion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive environment that encourages spiritual growth, lifelong learning, and acts of kindness. We are committed to fostering unity, promoting Islamic knowledge, and providing essential support to those in need.

Mosque Development

Our mosque development projects aim to enhance the facilities, create more learning spaces, and provide better amenities for our congregation. We believe in creating a spiritually uplifting space for worship and gathering.

Charity & Donation

Masjid Al-Noor actively engages in charitable efforts, collecting donations to support the needy, run relief programs, and extend a helping hand to those facing hardship. Together, we aim to make a meaningful difference.

Quran Learning

Our Quran Learning program is designed for individuals of all ages, focusing on authentic recitation, memorization, and understanding of the Quran. We believe in spreading the message of the Quran to inspire hearts and minds.

Read More

Hadith & Sunnah

Delve into the Hadith and Sunnah with our classes that explore the life and teachings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Learn how to incorporate the wisdom of the Prophet into your daily life and grow closer to Allah.

Parent Education

Our Parent Education program offers guidance on raising children with strong Islamic values, moral integrity, and a sense of community. We help parents strengthen their families spiritually and emotionally.
